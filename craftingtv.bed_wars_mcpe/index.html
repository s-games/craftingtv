BedWars

This is a very popular pvp map for team or single player play. Where players must fight and protect their beds at all costs. If any team or player loses their bed, they will not be able to respawn again and therefore lose the game.

Objective: Destroy the beds of your rivals so that when you kill them they cannot revive again. Remember that in the course of the game you will be able to collect minerals from the generators to buy weapons, food or blocks, with the villagers of your island.

Info:
- Self-repairing map in 50 seconds
- Custom trade
- Mineral generators with levels
- Play solo with 4 players or in 4 teams of 4 players
- Bugs in the map? run this command: /function reset
